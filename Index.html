Why encryption & decryption matter
Every file you own carries information worth protecting. CipherVault exists to make that protection simple, fast, and entirely under your control.

Why encrypt

Encryption scrambles a file's contents so it's unreadable to anyone without the correct key, keeping sensitive documents safe from prying eyes.

Why decrypt

Decryption reverses the process, restoring the original file exactly as it was, so authorized users can pick up right where they left off.

Local processing

Every operation runs on your own machine through our C engine. Files are never uploaded to a server, so nothing ever leaves your device.
